About Us

Our Story

Hashmi Fragrance was founded in 2019 with a vision to bring luxury fragrances to Pakistan at accessible prices. Our founder, Baqer Hashmi, was inspired by his travels through the Middle East in Holy land of Najaff, where he discovered the art of perfumery.

Today, we combine traditional fragrance techniques with modern Holy City Al-Madina, to create unique scents that stand out in the market.

Our mission is to provide premium quality perfumes that enhance your personality and leave a lasting impression.
