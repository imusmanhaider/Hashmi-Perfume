Our Story

Hashmi Frangness was founded in 2010 with a vision to bring luxury fragrances to Pakistan at accessible prices. Our founder, Baqer Hashmi, was inspired by his travels through the Middle East and Europe, where he discovered the art of perfumery.

Today, we combine traditional Eastern fragrance techniques with modern Western influences to create unique scents that stand out in the market.

Our mission is to provide premium quality perfumes that enhance your personality and leave a lasting impression.
