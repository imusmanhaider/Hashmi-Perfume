Shipping Policy

Last Updated: June 2023

1. Delivery Areas

We currently ship to all major cities and towns across Pakistan. Our delivery network covers:

  • All provincial capitals (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Peshawar, Quetta)
  • Major cities in all provinces
  • Selected rural areas (please check availability at checkout)

International shipping is available on select products. Please contact our customer service for international orders.

2. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and public holidays). Processing time may be longer during sale periods or promotional events.

Note: The processing time begins after payment verification for prepaid orders and order confirmation for Cash on Delivery orders.

3. Shipping Methods & Delivery Time

We partner with trusted courier services to ensure safe and timely delivery of your orders:

  • Standard Delivery: 2-3 business days (Rs. 150-200 depending on location)
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days (Rs. 300-400 depending on location)
  • Same Day Delivery: Available in select cities (Rs. 400-600) - Order must be placed before 12 PM

Delivery times are estimates and may vary depending on your location and courier service schedules.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on:

  • Delivery location
  • Package weight and dimensions
  • Selected shipping method

Free shipping is available on all orders over Rs. 5,000 (standard delivery only).

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your order:

  • Through the tracking link in your email
  • By logging into your account on our website
  • By contacting our customer support

6. Delivery Issues

If you encounter any issues with your delivery, please contact us immediately:

  • Undelivered packages: Contact us within 7 days of expected delivery date
  • Damaged packages: Do not accept the package if it appears damaged. Take photos and contact us immediately.
  • Wrong items received: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery

Important: We are not responsible for delays caused by incorrect shipping information, customs clearance, or circumstances beyond our control (natural disasters, strikes, etc.).
